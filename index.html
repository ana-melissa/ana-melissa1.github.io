About Me

Introduction

I grew up in Big Bear City, CA. My family holds a very special place in my heart, and they are incredibly important to me. My dad works at our local airport and my mom works as a realtor. I have two wonderful sisters and two adorable dogs. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz, with an expected graduation date in June 2024.

I work as an Oakes Student Life Assistant (Neighborhood Assistant NA). I live on campus and manage lock outs, answer calls, schedule meetings, and work alongside Oakes College staff. I have developed solid relationships with students, faculty, and staff. I am a member of UCSC's Computer Networking Student Association (CNSA). My coursework has encompassed a wide range of subjects including networking, computer systems, data structures, database systems, computer system design, and natural language processing. I plan to complete Advanced Computer Networks (CSE 151), Network Programming (CSE 156), and Computer Security (CSE 132) before I graduate in June 2024. I am actively building a solid foundation in network engineering and cybersecurity and am excited about the opportunity to contribute my developing skills to the safety of our nation.

Interests and Hobbies

My hobbies include backpacking, skiing, golfing, and guitar. I love Santa Cruz so when I'm not working on my studies I explore! My friend and I have taken up the hobby of backpacking which has allowed for many adventures. Recently we backpacked Sykes Hot Springs right after the big storm (Jan. 2023) so we had it all to ourselves along with some slippery trails. We also recently backpacked in the beautiful and untouched Henry Coe Park.

Goals

I am excited for this upcoming year because I will be wrapping up my experience at UCSC and moving into the real world. My main goal is to start getting experience and I hope to do that before I graduate and immediately after I graduate. I am on the lookout for internships/experiences. With my passion for computer science along with my dedication and strong work ethic, I think I could make a meaningful impact.
